# Pilot 1 — 60-Minute Walkthrough Script

## Session outcome

By minute 60, the pilot team will have uploaded one real client pack, validated its mappings, reviewed evidence-linked findings, recorded at least one review decision, tested manager/partner controls, exported a review pack and captured structured feedback.

## Roles

- **Facilitator:** ClosePilot pilot owner
- **Operator:** pilot preparer or manager sharing their screen
- **Reviewer:** manager
- **Decision-maker:** partner
- **Note-taker:** records defects, false positives, missed issues and timing

## Agenda

| Time | Activity | Acceptance evidence |
|---:|---|---|
| 0–5 | Introductions, scope and safety | Client/period confirmed; stop conditions understood |
| 5–10 | Sign in and orient the workspace | Correct firm/client and named users confirmed |
| 10–20 | Upload the finance pack | Files accepted; types and row counts reviewed |
| 20–28 | Validate mappings and data gates | Mapping decisions and any warnings recorded |
| 28–40 | Review findings and evidence | Three findings sampled; correctness decisions recorded |
| 40–48 | Evidence and review workflow | Assignment/evidence request plus resolution or false-positive action completed |
| 48–54 | Manager review and partner gates | Manager action recorded; blockers understood |
| 54–57 | Export the review pack | CSV/JSON or selected pack downloaded and checked |
| 57–60 | Feedback and next decision | Scores captured; blocker and owner agreed |

Total: **60 minutes**.

## Facilitator script

### 0–5 minutes — Frame the pilot

Say: “Today we are testing whether ClosePilot helps your team complete a trustworthy review. We are testing the product, not your staff. Please challenge every mapping, calculation and finding.”

Confirm:

- the signed pilot scope and nominated client;
- that no prohibited data is being uploaded;
- that professional judgement remains with the firm; and
- the security, data-isolation and material-total stop conditions.

### 5–10 minutes — Access and orientation

Ask the operator to sign in, identify the firm and client, and locate **Upload Finance Pack**, **Finance Review**, **Assurance Engine**, **Review Pack** and **Settings**.

Observe whether navigation needs explanation. Do not rescue the operator immediately; hesitation is useful product evidence.

### 10–20 minutes — Upload

Ask the operator to upload the agreed period's files together. Record upload duration and accepted/rejected files.

For each file confirm:

- detected document type;
- likely accounting source;
- row count; and
- whether the result is plausible to the operator.

Stop if the wrong client is shown or a material file is misclassified without a safe correction route.

### 20–28 minutes — Mapping and validation

Review mapping confidence, mandatory fields, numeric validation and trial-balance integrity. Ask the operator to explain why each warning can or cannot be accepted.

Record any manual mapping. A mapping is not trusted merely because ClosePilot generated findings.

### 28–40 minutes — Findings and evidence

Open the highest-severity queue. Sample:

1. one reconciliation finding;
2. one AR/AP or cash finding; and
3. one VAT, controls or data-quality finding.

For each ask:

- Is the finding correct?
- Is the severity proportionate?
- Can you trace the amount to source rows and calculation?
- What would you do in your normal review?
- Is anything material missing?

Record correct, false-positive, unclear and missed-issue counts.

### 40–48 minutes — Evidence lifecycle

Complete at least two actions:

- assign a finding;
- request evidence;
- attach or review evidence;
- resolve with a reason;
- accept risk; or
- mark false positive with an explanation.

Confirm the activity history explains who did what and why.

### 48–54 minutes — Manager and partner controls

Have the manager approve, return or escalate one suitable item. Open partner sign-off and read each blocking gate aloud.

Do not force a successful sign-off. A correctly blocked pack is a successful control test.

### 54–57 minutes — Export

Export the findings CSV and review-pack/evidence JSON or selected report. Confirm the client, period, findings, decisions, evidence and sign-off state agree with the on-screen review.

### 57–60 minutes — Close

Capture the eight ratings in the feedback form. Ask:

1. “Would you use this on another client next week?”
2. “What must change before you would?”

Agree one of:

- proceed to a second file;
- repeat after pilot-blocker fixes; or
- pause the pilot.

Name an owner and due date for every blocker. Schedule a 30-minute follow-up within seven days.

## Session acceptance record

- [ ] Correct firm, client and period confirmed.
- [ ] Upload classification and mappings reviewed.
- [ ] Source totals plausibility-checked.
- [ ] Three evidence-linked findings sampled.
- [ ] False positives and missed issues recorded.
- [ ] At least one evidence/review action completed.
- [ ] Manager and partner controls exercised.
- [ ] Review pack exported and checked.
- [ ] Feedback form completed.
- [ ] Next decision, owner and date agreed.
